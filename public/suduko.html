How to Play Mini Sudoku!

🎯 Your Goal

Fill empty cells with numbers! Every row, column, and smaller outline box must contain all numbers exactly once (1-4 for 4x4 grids, 1-6 for 6x6 grids, and 1-9 for 9x9 grids).

⚠️ Rules to Remember

  • Avoid repeating the same number in any row, column, or outline box.
  • Incorrect placements are automatically highlighted in red.
  • Use Notes mode to jot down possible ideas for any cell!
