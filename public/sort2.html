🧠 How to Play Sort & Think
🎮 Simple Steps:
  • Drag each item from the bottom and drop it into its correct category box.
  • Sort all items correctly before the timer hits 0 to complete the level!
  • Avoid wrong placements (the boxes will shake and make a warning sound).
  • Complete levels quickly to earn up to 3 stars and unlock cool badges!
🪙 Coins & Rewards:
  • Solve levels to earn coins based on difficulty:
  • Levels 1-10 (Easy): 15 Coins (15s Timer)
  • Levels 11-20 (Medium): 30 Coins (35s Timer)
  • Levels 21-30 (Medium to Hard): 45 Coins (45s Timer)
  • Levels 31-40 (Hard): 65 Coins (60s Timer)
  • Levels 41-50 (Very Hard): 85 Coins (75s Timer)
  • Stars are awarded based on remaining time:
  • ⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars: Solve with >50% time left
  • ⭐⭐ 2 Stars: Solve with >20% time left
  • ⭐ 1 Star: Solve with ≤20% time left
Earn coins and redeem them for discounts on BeCre8v products like RoboCoders, Spin Genius, etc.
