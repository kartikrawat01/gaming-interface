🎬 How to Play?
1
Add frames using the + button in the left filmstrip to build your animation sequence.
2
Draw frame by frame using Brush, Pencil, Shapes, Fill, Eyedropper and Eraser tools.
3
Use Duplicate Frame to copy the current frame and make small changes for smooth motion.
4
Use the Camera tool to capture real-world photos and turn them into stop motion animations.
5
Enable Onion Skin to see previous frames while drawing and create smoother animations.
6
Adjust FPS (Frames Per Second) to control how fast your animation plays.
7
Use Zoom controls for detailed drawing and precise editing.
8
Click Preview to watch your stop motion animation before saving or exporting.
9
Save, Export GIF, or Upload your creation when your animation is ready.
