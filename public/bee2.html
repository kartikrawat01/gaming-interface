🐝 How to Play

  1. Visit all flowers (🌸, 🌹, 🌻, 🌼) to collect nectar.
  2. Avoid unnecessary moves to keep your rating high.
  3. Reach the Hive (🏠) after collecting all nectar.
  4. Obstacles: Leaves (🍃/🍂), Water/Ocean (🌊), Trees (🌳), Cactus (🌵), and Rocks (🪨) cannot be crossed. The bee cannot pass through these obstacles.
  5. ⏳ Timer: Advanced levels (11–15) have a countdown timer (averaging 70 seconds) - help the bee reach home before time runs out!
  6. Use hints wisely when you get stuck.
  7. Complete levels in fewer moves to earn more coins.

✨ Special Items:

  • 🦋 Butterfly: Gives you an extra hint!
  • 🍯 Honey Pot: Gives you +15 Coins!
  • 🌟 Golden Blinking Flower: Gives you +10 Coins! (Note: It must still be visited and collected as part of the level!)
