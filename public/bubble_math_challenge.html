🎮 How to Play Bubble Math!

💡 Gameplay Rules

  • What to do: Solve the math equations inside the bubbles.
  • Complete Level: Pop the bubbles in order (either smallest-to-largest or largest-to-smallest, check the instruction bar!).
  • What to avoid: Avoid clicking a bubble out of order or running out of time.
  • To Win: Complete all 25 levels successfully!

🪙 Coins & Rewards

  • Levels 1 to 5: Earn 20 coins on completion
  • Levels 6 to 10: Earn 20 coins on completion
  • Levels 11 to 20: Earn 35 coins on completion
  • Levels 21 to 25: Earn 45 coins on completion
  • Scores: Receive +10 points for each correct pop and +50 bonus points on level complete!
  • Penalties: No coin penalties, but wrong moves or timeouts force you to restart the level.
