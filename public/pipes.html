How to Play!

Learning instructions made very simple

1

Click or tap a pipe piece to turn it around. Each click turns the pipe 90 degrees!

2

Create a complete path connecting the blue water pump 🚰 to all dry flower pots 🪵.

3

Press the huge Start Water Flow button to test your system!

4

Solve with as few moves as possible to win 3 Gold Stars! ⭐
